Kupzit® Bench Trial Worksheet

Use this worksheet to determine the correct dosage of Kupzit® for hydrogen sulfide removal in wine.

Bench Trial Protocol

1. Sample Preparation:

  • Collect at least 250–500 mL of affected wine per trial.
  • Keep a control sample (0 ppm) for comparison.

2. Kupzit® Slurry Preparation:

  • Mix 1 g of Kupzit® in 10 mL of wine (1:10 ratio, 10% stock solution).
    • *about 1ml-2ml of stock will be needed per lot.
  • Stir well for homogeneity.

3. Dosage and Mixing:

  • Prepare test samples with 50, 100, 150, and 200 ppm of Kupzit®.
  • Calculate the exact gram dosage using the calculator below.
  • Add slurry to each sample and stir thoroughly.

4. Settling & Evaluation:

  • Let samples sit for 30 min to 1 hour (or overnight if necessary).
  • Observe for sedimentation and changes in aroma.
  • Filter samples if needed for clarity.

5. Sensory & Chemical Testing:

  • Assess hydrogen sulfide reduction (1-5 scale).
  • Note any aroma changes and off-flavor removal.
  • Optionally test for residual copper levels.
